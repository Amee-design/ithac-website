# ITHAC Training Programs

ITHAC and its partners offer **15 professional training programs** designed for use on websites, brochures, partnership presentations, and training catalogs.

Each program advances career readiness and supports ITHAC's mission to empower through technology, creativity, and innovation. ITHAC provides structured, short-cycle professional training with mentorship opportunities, industry exposure, and practical skills in technology, innovation, media, and strategic communication.

## All Programs Include

- Instructor-guided sessions
- Hands-on assignments
- Mentorship opportunities
- Portfolio development
- Networking within the ecosystem
- Certificate of completion
