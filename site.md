# Site Architecture

Pages and routes available currently in the Next.js ITHAC project:

- `/` : **Home** - The digital gateway.
- `/about-us` : **About Us** - Information on ITHAC vision, mission, and team.
- `/services` : **Services** - Programs and Infrastructural innovation hubs.
- `/hub` : **Hub & Community** - The central hub dashboard for the ecosystem.
- `/communities` : **Communities** - Discover specialized multidisciplinary groups: Developers, Designers, Hub, and Creatives.
- `/collaboration` : **Collaboration** - Project initiation, WhatsApp links, and connection forms.
